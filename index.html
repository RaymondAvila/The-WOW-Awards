The WOW Awards

The World of Wonder (WOW) Award is awarded to the most precocious, prestegious, and ingenuitive entrepreneurs from around the world. Each will be recognized for their impact on humanity, the world, and the issue that they are targeting. At The WOW Awards we try to bring light to these issues and give recognization to these young ingenuitive men, women, and children for their achievements, dreams, and creativety. The winner of each catogory will receive funding to countinue their research for the next 5 years and to maintain a healthy life style.
